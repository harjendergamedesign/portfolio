I have been passionate about game design since childhood. My dream is to create revolutionary open-world life experience games that players remember for years.

Instead of simply designing gameplay, I design emotions, worlds, systems, progression, player psychology, exploration, and meaningful experiences.

One of my greatest strengths is transforming raw ideas into complete Game Design Documents — clear blueprints that communicate gameplay systems, mechanics, progression, and player journeys to a full studio team.
